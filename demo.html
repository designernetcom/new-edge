about us
Empowering Industries with Cutting-Edge Solutions

New Edge Technologies, is a global EPC and turnkey project management company head quartered in Pune, Maharashtra. We offer single point responsibility solutions and services for engineering and construction of industrial plants, from concept to commissioning. New Edge has been efficaciously providing EPC services across a wide spectrum of sectors such as Power Plant, Distilleries, Sugar Industry, Water & Waste Management Solutions, Oil & Gas, Chemical & Fertilizer Industry, Pharmaceuticals, Solar, Paper & Pulp and Textile Industry.

New Edge is known for their technology solutions & engineering services to deliver projects with best in class quality, on-time delivery and completion within budget. The company is also a green field solution provider; providing technology, technique layout and engineering, supply, construction, commissioning and kind of complete solutions to various core industry sectors.
